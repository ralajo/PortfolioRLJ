About Me

I'm Rachel Jones, a senior majoring in Human-Computer Interaction and Business Administration at Carnegie Mellon University.

Previously, I worked the a UX Designer for a startup, where I helped to transform the idea that there may be a room for increased efficiency in personal training into a funded business with a design that has since been developed and brought to commercial use.

Last summer, I interned as a Cognitive Systems Engineer at Resilient Cognitive Solutions in Pittsburgh where I used my understanding of design, cognitive psychology, and business to improve complex decision support systems.

When I am not designing you can find me running on the track and cross country teams.

I am passionate about accessibility as I see the created digital world as an opportunity to create a more inclusive society.I strive to learn and push myself every day to become a more effective and innovative problem solver and help create products and services that positively impact society.
